About Me

Hello, I am Md. Iqramul Hoque. I am currently a PhD candidate with a strong passion for leveraging data-driven approaches to solve complex industrial and scientific challenges. My academic journey began in Industrial and Production Engineering, where I developed a deep interest in the intersection of machine learning, advanced manufacturing, and operations research.

I have cultivated expertise in areas such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), and Digital Twin technologies.

Throughout my academic and research career, I have had the privilege of working under the guidance of esteemed faculty members at SUST. My goal is to contribute to impactful research that bridges theoretical understanding and industrial application.
